Dahlias (Dahlia) are a genus of flowering plants known for their vibrant and diverse blooms. They are perennial herbaceous plants with tuberous roots, producing large, intricate flowers that come in a wide range of colors including red, pink, orange, yellow, and white. The flowers are often characterized by their bold, symmetrical petals and striking forms, making them a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
